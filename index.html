Early thought on AI wanna share with you

TensorBlock is building the decentralized AI blockchain platform for Self-Organizing Intelligence.

Slogan: The Endgame of AGI: Open Source Models, Evolution Through Diversity.

AI inference today mirrors the early days of distributed systems. While machines can now connect to networks effortlessly, the software framework needed to coordinate them for distributed tasks remains absent. TensorBlock is here to bridge that gap.

We believe open-source LLMs are the ultimate destination. Just as Linux became the dominant OS for servers and laid the foundation for Android, we see open-source as the future of AI.

Our goal is to incentivize people to contribute their resources. We’re starting with GPUs, but we’ll eventually unlock more—residential IPs, bandwidth, CPUs, RAM, and storage.

We believe diversity fuels evolution. While many great models exist on Hugging Face, they lack true vitality. TensorBlock breathes life into them.
